This folder contains the processed data used in West et al. (2023), "Action needed to make carbon offsets from forest conservation work for climate change mitigation," published by Science.
Google Earth Engine codes available at https://earthengine.googlesource.com/users/srifai/west-gfc
See the article's Supplementary Information for the references on additional publicly available datasets used in the analyses.
Please report potential data issues to Dr. Thales A. P. West at t.a.pupowest@vu.nl
